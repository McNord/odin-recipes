Freyja's Golden Nectar Mead

Description

Indulge in the divine essence of Freyja's Golden Nectar Mead, a magical brew fit for the gods.

Crafted with the finest honey from the Fields of Folkvangr and infused with the enchantment of Norse mythology, this sparkling mead is a delightful elixir that captures the spirit of Asgard.
Perfect for any feast or celebration, this golden nectar promises a taste of the divine.

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs of Honey from the Fields of Folkvangr
  • 1 gallon of Water from the Well of Urd
  • 1 packet of Yeast from the Magic Cauldron

Equipment:

  • 1-gallon fermentation jug or carboy
  • Airlock and stopper
  • Siphon tube (for transferring the mead)
  • Sanitizer (to clean all equipment)

Instructions:

  1. Sanitize your equipment:
    • Clean and sanitize all your equipment thoroughly to keep any unwanted spirits away

  2. Mix the honey and water:
    • In a large pot, warm half of the water (do not boil). Stir in the honey until it dissolves completely.
    • Place the jug in a cool, dark place and let it ferment for about 4-6 weeks. You'll see bubbles in the airlock, a sign that the magic is working.

  3. Add the yeast:
    • Sprinkle the yeast into the jug and give it a gentle stir to wake up the magic.

  4. Ferment:
    • Attach the airlock and stopper to the jug. Fill the airlock with water.
    • Place the jug in a cool, dark place and let it ferment for about 4-6 weeks. You'll see bubbles in the airlock, a sign that the magic is working.

  5. Bottle and enjoy:
    • Once the mead is ready, siphon it into clean bottles. Seal the bottles and store them in a cool, dark place.
    • Chill the mead before serving and enjoy Freyja's Golden Nectar Mead, a divine drink fit for the gods!

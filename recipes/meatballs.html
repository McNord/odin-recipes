Thor's Mighty Mjolnir Meatballs

Description

Unleash the power of the gods with Thor's Mighty Mjolnir Meatballs!

These legendary meatballs, shaped like the mighty Mjolnir, are made with a savory blend of ground beef, breadcrumbs, and a touch of garlic and herbs.
Cooked to golden perfection, these meatballs are a hearty and delicious dish that will bring the strength of Thor to your table.

Perfect for any feast, these mighty meatballs are sure to be a hit with gods and mortals alike!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of Ground Beef of the Gods
  • 1/2 cup of Breadcrumbs from the Halls of Valhalla
  • 1/4 cup of Milk of the Sacred Cow
  • 1 Egg of the World Serpent
  • 1/4 cup of Onions of the Nine Realms
  • 2 cloves of Garlic of the Giants
  • 1 tsp of Salt from the Sea of Jotunheim
  • 1/2 tsp of Pepper from the Fires of Muspelheim
  • 1 tsp of Herbs of the Earth
  • 1 tbsp of Oil of the Lightning Bolt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the meatball mixture:
    • In a large bowl, combine the Ground Beef of the Gods, Breadcrumbs from the Halls of Valhalla, Milk of the Sacred Cow, Egg of the World Serpent, Onions of the Nine Realms, Garlic of the Giants, Salt from the Sea of Jotunheim, Pepper from the Fires of Muspelheim, and Herbs of the Earth.
      Mix well until all ingredients are evenly distributed.

  2. Form the meatballs:
    • Shape the mixture into small, Mjolnir-shaped meatballs. You can use a small hammer-shaped cookie cutter or mold to give them their iconic shape.

  3. Cook the meatballs:
    • Heat the Oil of the Lightning Bolt in a large skillet over medium heat.
    • Add the meatballs to the skillet and cook until they are golden brown on all sides and cooked through, about 10-12 minutes.

  4. Serve:
    • Transfer the meatballs to a serving plate. Serve with a side of mashed potatoes or a hearty gravy for a truly mighty meal.

Enjoy your Thor's Mighty Mjolnir Meatballs, a powerful and delicious dish fit for a god!
